Error Codes for Images
======================
ERR001 = Error while processing the image
ERR002 = Image is not a Pan card or Aadhaar card

Error Codes for Pan Card
========================
PANERR001 = Pan card Rejected. All required information was not available.
PANERR002 = Error while extracting the first 5 characters of Pan Card Number.
PANERR003 = Error while extracting the first 6 characters of DOB from PAN Card

Errot codes for Pan Card
========================
AADERR001 = Aadhaar card Rejected. All required information was not available
